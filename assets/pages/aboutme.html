About Me

Hello, I'm Joshua and this is my personal mission statement:

1. I will give all glory to God through my heart, soul, mind, and strength.
2. Defend those in need.
3. Build strength and gentleness in equal proportion.
4. Think independently.
5. Never lose a sense of humor.
6. Fear nothing but the Lord.
7. Don't allow time to limit generosity.
8. Hone the intentions, not the outcome.
9. Seek and merit outside help.
10. Listen twice, speak once, when it's important.
11. Sing through suffering and success.
12. Hold onto hope in His compassion and faithfulness.
